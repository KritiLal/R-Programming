# R-Programming
DATA ANALYSIS ON NETFLIX MOVIES AND TV  SHOWS USING R LANGUAGE
Introduction:
This dataset consists of listing of all the movies and Tv shows available on Netflix
along with details such as cast, directors, ratings, release year, a Unique Id for
movie/tv show, country where the movie/tv show was produced, date Tv
show/movie was added on Netflix and total duration, etc. This dataset analysis
helps you find the most popular and best rated Tv shows and movies according to
different contents available in different countries. And in this dataset, we will
analyze what does Netflix users prefer more- Tv shows or movies.

Objective:
1. We will visualize the proportion of Netflix content by type (Movie/Tv
shows).
2. We will visualize the Top 12 Countries by Amount of content they have
produced.
3.We will visualize Netflix&#39;s growth in amount of content as a function of
time.
4.We will visualize the amount of content added per month.
5. We will visualize the distribution of Netflix content by rating classes.
6. We will visualize movie duration in top 12 countries.
7. We will also visualize Tv shows duration (in seasons) in top 12 countries.
